Contribute an Application

Have some applications we don't? Upload your copies to the Observatory and help build a history of application data and signatures that benefits everyone.

Choose an APK file from your computer and click 'Upload' to submit the application to the Observatory.

All APKs and files uploaded to the Android Observatory are stored indefinitely on servers located inside the Carleton Computer Security Lab. APKs are kept for research purposes, and will never be shared, sold or distributed to any third-party. We do, however, make available the Android Observatory database which contains parsed-out metadata from uploaded APKs. Please see our Privacy Policy for more information on retention/distribution of your submission.
